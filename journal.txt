Phase B B1 — Example 1 reproduction (commit 5637771):
Wind power Markov chain example. Paper formula 'A_{ijk} = mu_1*Q[i,j] +
mu_2*Q[i,k] + mu_3*Q_i' is ambiguous (3 indices but tensor order = 4,
'Q_i' notation unclear).

Disambiguation via 6-candidate brute-force sweep with NNI 5-iter convergence
as oracle (paper Figure 1):
- m=3 candidates: ruled out by paper text 'order 4'
- m=4 stationary (l-dummy): 5 iter, lambda=15.7719, but rank-deficient in l
  axis (not a transition probability tensor)
- m=4 third_follower: 5 iter, lambda=15.9115, Raftery 1985 MTD standard form

third_follower selected. The third Q channel depends on the missing 4th
index l: A_{i,j,k,l} = mu_1*Q[i,j] + mu_2*Q[i,k] + mu_3*Q[i,l]. This matches
Raftery (J.R.S.S. 1985) ref [20] cited in paper §7 Example 1.

Reproduction confirmed:
- nit = 5 (paper Figure 1: NNI converges in 5 iterations)
- lambda_U = 15.911456053987
- final residual = 2.233e-16 (machine epsilon)
